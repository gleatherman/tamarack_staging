Michael’s investment banking career began with New York based Smith Barney in 1985. In 1992 he moved to Minneapolis to join the partners of Wessels, Arnold & Henderson, an institutional investment banking firm serving high growth / emerging market companies. During his career, he has banked clients in healthcare, technology, energy and manufacturing, underwriting IPO’s, raising private capital and advising on mergers and acquisitions. For the past 15 year he has focused exclusively on advising business owners in mergers and acquisitions.

Michael married Kathy in 1990 and they have two wonderful children – one in New York City and one in Montreal. Michael loves a fine restaurant and a good golf course but mostly a family reunion. He is a graduate of St. John’s College Annapolis and received his MBA from Columbia University. He has served on several charitable boards and served active duty in the Marine Corps in the late 70’s.

Why Tamarack?

Tamarack provides a more valuable service to our clients by combining our expertise in merger and acquisition execution with the insights of our industry operators. Our team of transactional and industry partners are experts at:

  • Understand your business
  • Accentuating the value of products and services
  • Identifying unique and likely buyers, and
  • Accessing the decision makers of those buyers

Our counsel includes this “seller’s perspective”, offering insights and guidance beyond deal terms.
