Why Tamarack?

You want an advisor that can make you shine brightly

Over the course of 35 years, we have executed a lot of Merger and acquisition transactions. These have been transactions executed from both large investment banking platforms and small boutique M&A advisors. What invariably distinguishes the quality of service provided is the ability to:

  • Truly understand a company’s business
  • Identify the unique and likely buyers, and
  • Gain access to those decision makers.

This is where we edge out the competition every time.

Our Industry Partners are entrepreneurs and executives who have started and grown businesses, fought and succeeded in the competitive environment you see every day, and lived the sale process with the scars to prove it. They are active and engaged and have vibrant networks that can be leveraged for you.
