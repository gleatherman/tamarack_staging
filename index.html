Michael’s investment banking career began at New York based Smith Barney in 1985. In 1992 he moved to Minneapolis to join the partners of Wessels, Arnold & Henderson, an institutional investment banking firm serving high growth / emerging market companies. During his career, he has specialized in healthcare, technology and industrials, underwriting IPO’s, raising private capital and advising on mergers and acquisitions. For the past 15 years he has focused exclusively on advising private business owners in mergers and acquisitions. He has sat on several charitable boards, include the United Health Foundation and Children’s Cancer Research.

Michael married Kathy in 1990 and they have two wonderful children – one in New York City and one in Montreal. Michael loves a fine restaurant and a good golf course but mostly a family reunion. Industry Partners

Our industry partners are an invaluable resource — entrepreneurs and business owners who have founded, grown and sold businesses just like yours. Your Tamarack team will quickly understand your business model and its KPIs, appreciate your markets and challenges, tailor your story to its highest value for prospective buyers and gain buyer access more quickly. Tamarack’s counsel and advice includes the “seller’s perspective” of operators who have sold their own businesses - their insights and guidance goes well beyond deal terms.

Your Tamarack team includes industry entrepreneurs and executives who have started, grown and sold businesses.

Why Tamarack?

Over the course of our careers, we have executed well over a hundred transactions at large investment banking platforms and small boutique M&A advisors. What we know is that the combination of transaction expertise with industry intelligence best serves the client. At Tamarack we can provide that combination. Most boutique M&A advisors simply don’t have the resources to do this well across a variety of industries.

Our expertise in M&A execution with our industry operator’s insight and intelligence lets us punch well above our weight.
