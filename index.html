Founders

Following extensive careers in investment banking and financial services, Michael Kessler and Larry Gamst recognized the need for a new approach to sell-side representation. Together they formed Tamarack Partners to combine their own deep transaction expertise with real business intelligence of owners and operators.

Michael began his career at Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Company in New York. In 1992 he moved to Minneapolis to join the partners of Wessels, Arnold & Henderson, LLC, an institutional brokerage and investment banking firm focused on high growth companies in technology and healthcare.

In 2002 he semi-retired to spend more time with his young children while advising a small group of business owners in the region as a board member and consultant. In 2008 he embarked on the full-time endeavor of advising small- and middle-market clients in the Midwest.

Michael married Kathy in 1990 and they have two wonderful children – one in New York City and one in Montreal. Michael loves a fine restaurant and a good golf course but mostly a family reunion. He is a graduate of St. John’s College Annapolis and the received his MBA from Columbia University. Why Tamarack?

You want an advisor that can make you shine brightly

Tamarack provides a more valuable service to our clients by combining our expertise in M&A execution with the intelligence of our Industry Partners. Our team of transactional and industry partners have is the ability to:

  • Truly understand the client’s business
  • Accentuate the value of products and services
  • Identify the unique and likely buyers, and
  • Access the decision makers at those buyers

Our Industry Partners are entrepreneurs and executives who have started and grown businesses, fought and succeeded in the competitive environment you see every day, and lived the sale process with the scars to prove it.

The expertise of our Industry Partners includes technology, healthcare and selected industrial sectors.

Industry Partners

Our industry partners have founded, grown and sold businesses. They are an invaluable resource who can quickly understand your business model and it’s KPI’s, appreciate your markets and challenges, and focus your story towards its highest value to perspective buyers. At the same time, their counsel to you as a seller can provide personal and unique guidance on a myriad of issues.
