Tamarack Partners

Transactional capability meets industry intelligence.

At Tamarack Partners, our focus and our expertise is representing business owners who want to realize the value they have built by selling all or a portion of their company. Our team includes seasoned industry partners who know your business.

Partners

Our investment banking expertise comes from 30+ years of merger and acquisition transactions, while our business insights are enhanced through industry partners. Our partners are successful health care, technology and manufacturing entrepreneurs and business executives who have founded, run and often sold businesses. They have valued operating and competitive insights and industry relationships well beyond a sell-side banker.

Together, the partners of Tamarack can bring a unique combination of the seasoned professional M&A experience and specific and relevant industry intelligence to our clients.

Why Tamarack? Because we field a more valuable team.

Over the course of 35 years, we have executed a lot of Merger and acquisition transactions. These have been transactions executed from both large investment banking platforms and small boutique M&A advisors. What invariably distinguishes the quality of service provided is the ability to:

  • Truly understand a company’s business
  • Identify the unique and likely buyers, and
  • Gain access to those decision makers.

This is where we edge out the competition every time.

Our Industry Partners are entrepreneurs and executives who have started and grown businesses, fought and succeeded in the competitive environment you see every day, and lived the sale process with the scars to prove it. They are active and engaged and have vibrant networks that can be leveraged for you.
